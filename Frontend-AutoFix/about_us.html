About Us

Welcome to AutoFix, your one-stop solution for effortless vehicle maintenance and repair services. Founded by Gyanendra Sharma, AutoFix was created with a vision to transform the traditional auto repair industry by integrating cutting-edge technology with the convenience of modern digital solutions. Our journey began as a group of passionate tech enthusiasts, all currently pursuing our Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA) in the 5th semester.

Our Core Team

  • Vansh Batra: A strategic thinker and problem solver with expertise in front-end development, ensuring a seamless user experience on our platform.
  • Kartik Gupta: A backend specialist, responsible for ensuring the robustness and efficiency of our systems.
  • Ajay Jha: A database wizard who guarantees that our platform can handle large volumes of data with accuracy and speed.
  • Vivek Gautam: A visionary in UI/UX design, crafting an intuitive and engaging interface for both customers and mechanics.

Our Mission

At AutoFix, our mission is to simplify the vehicle repair process by bridging the gap between vehicle owners and trusted mechanics. We aim to make vehicle maintenance:

  • Hassle-free: No more endless searches for a reliable mechanic or spending hours in a repair shop.
  • Efficient: By using our platform, customers can find and book a mechanic with just a few clicks.
  • Accessible: We are committed to ensuring that anyone, regardless of their location or vehicle type, can access top-notch repair services.

Why Choose AutoFix?

  • Easy-to-Use Platform: Our user-friendly web app allows you to quickly find nearby mechanics, book services, and even choose specific repair types based on your vehicle’s needs.
  • Trusted Mechanics: We work with a network of certified and experienced mechanics, ensuring that you receive reliable service every time.
  • Transparent Pricing: At AutoFix, we believe in transparency. You’ll know the estimated cost upfront, avoiding unexpected charges or hidden fees.
  • Customer-Centric Approach: Your satisfaction is our priority. We listen to feedback and continuously improve our platform to better serve our users.
  • Innovative Features: From real-time tracking to reminders for future maintenance, our platform is designed to make your vehicle care experience smoother than ever.

Our Vision

Our long-term vision is to become the leading platform for vehicle maintenance globally. We envision a future where AutoFix becomes synonymous with trust, efficiency, and innovation in the automotive services industry.

Building Lasting Relationships

At AutoFix, we believe in building strong relationships with both customers and mechanics. We aim to provide mechanics with opportunities to grow their business while offering customers an unparalleled service experience.

Join Us on This Journey

As we continue to grow and expand, we invite you to be a part of the AutoFix journey. Whether you’re a customer in need of a mechanic, or a mechanic looking to reach more clients, we are here to make your experience simpler and better.
